Akimbo H&K MP5K

Clip size: 15 Rounds X 2
Rate of Fire: 800 RPM X 2
Damage: Low

A great pair of SMGs, they are fast, and deadly. 
Effective for spraying and praying, these have a
relatively quick reload, so the spraying is
only shortly interrupted.

Changing modes switches between which mp5k
you have out. Both mp5ks need ammo to fire
in akimbo mode.
