H&K 33ka2 Sniper Rifle

Clip size: 25 Rounds
Rate of Fire: 600 RPM
Damage: High

The H&K33ka2 is a standard assault rifle. This 
gun has single, three round, and fully automatic
firing modes as well as a scope. It is a very 
flexible gun and when wielded by an experienced 
user it can be useful in many situations, be it 
sniping or close quarters battles. There is no 
secondary fire.

This rifle becomes very inaccurate during
sustained fire, so controlled bursts are essential 
for efficient use.

Use your bound zoom buttons to zoom in and
out.
