Tech Guru

Bonuses:
-Faster Cap-times
-Free Enhanced IR Goggles

Restrictions
-Must be wearing armor

Some soldiers undergo special training for the
more complicated activitives that happen
during a war. Things like hacking a computer
network, working with high-tech gadgets, and
other geeky things are the realm of the Tech
Guru.

Some paths to objectives can only be opened by
a tech guru. Use your objective info button to
find them.
