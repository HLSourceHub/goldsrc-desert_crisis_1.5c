Character Selection
Once you join a game in Desert Crisis, you will be presented with a lot of options, some of which, you have most likely never seen before.  First, you will be given a screen to choose a team or spectate.  It will also give you a little info about the map. 
After choosing your team, a list of your teams objectives will come up, once read click ok.

Choose an  armor class (this only determines your armor and speed), your weaponry, equipment and perk. Each team has four "classes", none, light, medium and heavy.  The right side of the screen will show a picture of the weapon, equipment, perk or armor highlighted, along with a description of it (note: some percs do not let you use certain guns or armor classes).  The top right hand corner of the screen is an overview of all of your selections.

None - Gives the player extra speed but no armor.
Light - Gives the player extra speed but little armor.
Medium - Average armor.
Heavy - Lower speed but gives full armor.

 
Next you will be able to customize the look of your player's head.  Click customize below random to begin.  With over 8000 different combinations, any player can look original.  After the head is customized click ok and you're ready to join the game.
