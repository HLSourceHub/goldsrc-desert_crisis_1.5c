Explanation in the Ingame section.
Command Menu
Team messages, map info, motions, choose equipment, choose teams, and server information.
CHANGE MODES
Using the change modes button has different effects depending on which weapon you have out. If you have for instance an MP51/0 or HK 33KA2 out, change modes will simply switch between single shot, three round burst and fully automatic. Yet if you have akimbo weapons out, change modes will switch between akimbo, left weapon, and right weapon. So instead of reloading your single pistol, if you have another one you can simply switch to that one instead. Be careful though because if you empty one and bring them both out akimbo, it will still be empty.
DASH
When you set your controls, you set a button to dash.  To use this, you hold the Dash button and you will move faster than normal.  The only disadvantages to dash are that you cannot jump and you cannot shoot while in dash mode.  It also uses up your adrenaline rather fast, so its best to use this wisely and in small bursts.
DROP CURRENT ITEM
Pressing this button will drop whatever weapon/item you have drawn at that moment.
DROP THE OBJECT
Use this to drop objects such as flags, and other 'objects' which are part of the map (NOT your weapons and equipment). This is useful for passing the object to a faster player.
INFARED GOGGLES
To turn these on use your flashlight button.  For more info go to the power meter part of the in Ingame section.
Objective Info
When held this will show you where objectives are located.
TOGGLE ITEMS
The toggle items button is for switching on/off items such as shields, anti grav, and sharpshooter.  For more info go to the power meter part of the in Ingame section.
Toggle QuickCSS
Lets you select weapons ingame much faster.
ZOOMING
Set your zoom controls, zoom in and zoom out.  Hold your respective zoom button (either in or out) to zoom and double tap zoom in to zoom in completely or zoom out to zoom out completely (for the hk and em sniper rifles only).
Advanced Controls
Last Used weapons - Quick key to choose your last used weapon.
Commit Suicide - Quick key to kill yourself.
Select Chosen Melee - Quick key to select the melee you chose.
Select Chosen Secondary - Quick key to select the secondary weapon you chose.
Select Chosen Primary - Quick key to select the primary weapon you chose.
Select Chosen Equipment - Quick key to select the equipment you chose.
