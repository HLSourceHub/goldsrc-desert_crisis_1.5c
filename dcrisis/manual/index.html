UP-KO (United Peace-Keeping Organization) was dedicated to bringing order and prosperity to the world. In their struggles for peace they began gathering the worlds greatest minds to create "weapons of peace" with which they planned to help resolve issues and protect the weak. The organization was a great success and peace soon spread the globe.  As the years passed UP-KO began to create trouble. Where they once stood for peace, small factions within the organization began using their technology to bring themselves further power and strength. They forced scientists to help them create new weapons and began to fight amongst themselves. UP-KO began to lose membership from small countries who felt they were being pushed around.

     At this point the united leaders turned against the very vocal United States. President Wilson Bernhardt had begun to publicly accuse powerful members of UP-KO of treason and acts against humanity. Being one of the largest members of UP-KO, and one of the most influential, these actions led to open revolt as people began to rally and strike back against UP-KO. The organization was in trouble...

     After a few months the US broke off from UP-KO and declared openly that no more selfish actions would be tolerated. This caused more countries to abandon UP-KO until it basically became a conglomerate of European, Middle Eastern and Asian forces. Now on the brink of war the two sides have abandoned global weapons in their conflict fearing MAD (Mutually Assured Destruction) if they used these weapons.

     Rising from the ashes of the crisis, the Scourge of the Desert (otherwise known as the "Scorpion's Sting") began their crusade towards peace. Funded by untold millions, this group of freedom fighters believes that neither side cares for true peace and fight to liberate themselves and their brethren. The Scourge have freed hundreds of scientists and researchers from the clutches of UP-KO and have found themselves powerful allies. Amassing quite exclusive advances in cybernetic prosthesis they have become a force to reckon with. 

     The world now is truly at war as everyone claims to be fighting for peace. Each side draws hundreds of new recruits each day... Which side are you willing to fight for...

Desert Crisis - Timeline

2015 - Israel deploys its first nuclear warheads. Egypt deploys theirs four months later. Tension rises across the world as bloodshed continues in the Middle East.

2018 - American scientists solve the problems with fusion power. The first fusion reactor is deployed two years later near Los Angeles.

2021 - Nuclear weapons are used against the Egyptians by Israeli forces. The Egyptians strike back with their remaining nuclear arsenal two days later. Approximately fifteen warheads were fired in this period, the Israeli and Egyptian forces are devastated. Nearby country's are harmed greatly due to the fallout and the massive increase in refugees. Combat ceases between Israeli and Egyptian forces who are now busy trying to save their countries.

2024 - Russia develops plasma weapon technology, it is highly unstable at the time. Russia sells this technology to the a variety of European countries.

2025 - The world's oil supply is almost entirely exhausted, American scientists make massive profits off of building fusion reactors all over the world for massive fees. Protests over this price gouging are heard all over the world.

2027 - US embassies in thirteen countries are burned to the ground by protesters. The US agrees to build their reactors for far lower costs.

2030 - All of Europe becomes the "European Alliance", effectively one country. All protests are silenced swiftly by agents of unknown affiliation. No documentation of these protests ever appear in any mainstream media outlet. Underground rumors state that this is the working of the Illuminati. 

2032 - A Russian chemical and Biological weapons facility is raided by an unknown group and every person there is killed. A multitude of weapons and virii are missing. Very few are ever recovered, paranoia rises worldwide as security precautions rise to unheard of heights. Later that year an entire town is exposed to a psychotropic agent in their water supply and goes insane. Thousands are put in mental institutions.

2034 - Nuclear war breaks out between Pakistan and India, the damage is minor compared to the Egypt/Israel nuclear exchange, but this causes vast amounts of fallout to fall across the globe.

2035 - The environment begins to falter significantly, temperatures are rising noticeably each year. The rain forests are almost entirely stripped. Protesters march in the streets of London for three days. The head of a logging company in Brazil is murdered and found nailed to a tree with his flesh removed. In his hands is a message that reads, "Cease the destruction" with the insignia of a scorpion.

2037 - Fighting resumes in the middle east when a hospital in Israel is bombed.

2039 - Anti-gravity technology is unveiled in Germany by a small research team. The team disappears three weeks later and their entire research facility is found empty. There is no evidence as to what happened to them. Buenos Aires is abandoned after a terrorist attack with a biological weapon, over 50,000 people die from its effects. The attack was credited to an organization called "Bringers de La Entropía", dedicated to bringing chaos to the world. Disease begins to spread across South America, looting and violence ensues. Quarantines are placed in hundreds of areas in South America.

2040 - The European nations encourage the world to form a new organization dedicated to bringing peace. Meanwhile, conflict continues in the Middle East, which is now in a massive recession due to the oil shortage and the perseverance of war.

2042 - The United States unveils energy shield technology. At first this is used primarily on fighter jets, but it is soon adapted for squad combat.

2044 - The cure is found for the epidemic in South America. European agents distribute the vaccine. Some of the countries are so grateful that they join the European Alliance, vowing to uphold worldwide peace.

2045 - The European Alliance becomes the "United Peace-Keeping Organization", dedicated to peace, prosperity and justice. A multitude of countries join the organization, only Russia,  Brazil,  Japan, India and Australia do not join (as well as a few smaller countries). Peace spreads the globe as conflict in the Middle East has finally come to what seems to be an end. Technology is shared freely among UP-KO nations and economic recessions become a thing of the past. Fusion power permeates the world and the energy crisis is solved.

2047 - UP-KO scientists unveil the first power armor suits. Using miniature fusion reactors and reinforced light-weight alloys, they are the most advanced personal protection systems to date. UP-KO squads are using this technology within the year. Some of the models incorporate anti-gravity technology.

2048 - All of the remaining smaller countries, India, Brazil and Australia  join UP-KO due to their economies faltering and terrorist attacks on their relatively unguarded nations. None of these terrorist attacks are ever credited to any known organizations. Laser weapon technology is deployed among UP-KO units which was recently refined by Chinese UP-KO scientists. Russia and Japan remain separate from UP-KO.

2049 - Unknown terrorist attacks increase on Russia and Japan, rumors spread about UP-KO's involvement in these attacks. Sporadic strikes against UP-KO are reported across the world. An UP-KO research facility in Sweden is assaulted, forty scientists are missing as well a large amount of classified technology.

2051 - The covert assaults against UP-KO by this unknown organization continue as people begin to doubt UP-KO's power. A few news reports get out on an "Operation Black Shield" in which UP-KO agents invaded Moscow and assassinated various government agents in hopes of persuading Russia to join the organization due to their lack of security. Russia becomes enraged and calls for countries to leave the corrupt organization, UP-KO says it was an isolated incident and was not authorized by the head UP-KO officials. 

2052 - An UP-KO base in Iran is attacked and destroyed. Burned into the roof of the facility is the logo of a scorpion. Reports are released across the world about the organization that is now known as the "Scourge of the Desert". Details are sketchy and no one is able to find where this organization is based. Two months later, a city in Japan is attacked for "treason against UP-KO" by UP-KO, a force of SotD shows up within hours and defends the city successfully. It is unclear how UP-KO was defeated, large squads are found dead without a single shot fired from their weapons. Japan and Russia proclaim their appreciation for the SotD's efforts against UP-KO. The United States breaks off from UP-KO and President Wilson Bernhardt publicly announces that he believes UP-KO is a corrupt and vile organization run by conspirators interested in nothing but advancing their own agendas. Two days later there is an attempted assassination of the President, Canada secedes from UP-KO that same day. Canada then joins the USA to create the "American Block", a new force opposed to UP-KO's way of ruling the world.

2053 - Violence erupts worldwide as the American Block and UP-KO clash. USA raids UP-KO bases worldwide in a campaign to bring down UP-KO. UP-KO ramps up its research in order to overcome USA through force and technology. A weapons manufacturing facility in Connecticut is destroyed by the SotD and melted into a metal wall is the message, "Cease the destruction." A variety of countries split off from UP-KO and request the protection of the American Block This is the current time in which DC takes place.

  • USA is attempting to steal UP-KO's recently developed matter-destabilization cannon. The cannon is currently in two parts, the cannon itself is in a research facility on an island in Indonesia, and the computer that controls the cannon is in Paris. USA intends to steal this weapon and assault UP-KO bases with it.
  • UP-KO is systematically assaulting USA facilities in an attempt to cripple USA's rebel attacks against UP-KO.
  • SotD is infiltrating and sabotaging USA and UP-KO bases.

Once UP-KO's forces acheive this, they intend to destroy NORAD, the White House, and all military bases.
  • USA is attempting to hijack UP-KO supply trains.
  • UP-KO is searching out and attacking SotD cave complexes in Germany.
  • A weapons research facility in Sacramento is being attacked by SotD soldiers.
  • USA is attacking a convention center in England full of UP-KO diplomats and generals.
  • UP-KO and USA are fighting for control of various cities in Egypt.
  • USA has discovered an UP-KO base hidden on a mountain in Norway, they intend to assault it once they have recovered the weapon.
  • SotD is stopping UP-KO from assaulting a city Russia.
  • USA is attempting to steal vehicles from an UP-KO airbase in Sweden.

Desert Crisis - UP-KO History

An extension of the European Alliance which formed in 2030, solidifying the European alliances completely. Engineered by Illuminati agents, this Alliance soon began incorporating other countries promising an end to the violence. In 2045, the European Alliance was renamed the "United Peace-Kepping Organization" and spread faster than ever before. UP-KO's technology is some of the best in the world, with power armor, anti-gravity, plasma weapons, laser weapons, and even portable railguns. At present time, UP-KO is primarily composed of the European, African, Asian and South American nations. They are by far the most powerful organization in the world, but their influence is beginning to falter as more and more countries break off. The European countries remain the most loyal to UP-KO's cause. UP-KO's mantra is "Peace, Prosperity and Justice."

Desert Crisis - USA History

The American Block consists of the United States, Canada and a variety of smaller countries including: New Zealand, Uruguay, South Africa, and Greenland. Formed in 2052 when the USA and Canada both broke off from UP-KO, the USA is a formidable force. Their research program is behind UP-KO's, but they have some of the best scientists in the world working as hard as they can to develop new weapons and defense technology. USA gains support every day as more countries break off from UP-KO. USA is still the country that holds the most fusion technology, as well as energy shield technology. Tactical assaults on UP-KO research facilities are attempts to make sure this remains so.

Desert Crisis - SotD History

The Scourge of the Desert was first founded in 2033 by a group of Egyptian cybernetics researchers, and their first known action was the slaying of a logging company leader in Brazil. The organization did not become publicly known until 2052 when they destroyed an UP-KO base in Iran. The Scourge of the Desert has many exclusive technological advances. Many of SotD's researchers are scientists liberated from UP-KO's tyrannical grip. The SotD has near exclusive advances in cybernetic prosthesis and cloaking. The SotD strives to end world conflict and believes that both UP-KO and the American Block are doing nothing but causing violence. The SotD's tactical strikes are aimed at stopping their military forces and preventing global destruction. The organization is very secretive and has never had a single agent captured alive. The organization is very rigid and to an outsider it would seem religious in its rituals.

The technology of the Scourge of the Desert is very complex and multi-faceted. Every SotD soldier on the field is cybernetically enhanced to at least some extent. Their cybernetic enhancements include, but are not limited to: cybernetic endoskeletons, enhanced eyes that interface with custom weapons to allow "smart aiming" (as well as multiple vision modes on the fly), retractable blades, and the ability to harness and expel electrical charges.

The Scourge of the Desert is composed of approximately 2 million members, with outposts in every country in the world. The command structure in the SotD is very rigid, and they do not tolerate anyone in the organization who does not follow the rules, they can not afford to.

Desert Crisis - Other World Factions

Russia - One of the most heavily assaulted countries by UP-KO. They are a proud but oppressed people. Loosely allied with the American Block, they oppose UP-KO very vocally. Unknown to the world, Russia has made impressive advances in AI technology and is working to deploy an automated army to fight back against UP-KO.

Japan - The other country which never joined UP-KO, but they were small enough and quiet enough to largely avoid UP-KO's wrath until the direct assault in 2052. Japan's forces are small but highly trained, and have weapons such as ion cannons and sonic weaponry. Japan also has armor technology that rivals UP-KO's power armor, being far lighter and more maneuverable and incorporating a variety of chemicals used on its wearer to enhance their combat effectiveness.

Bringers de La Entropía - An organization dedicated to bringing chaos to the world. Responsible for the raid on the Russian chemical and biological weapons facility in 2032 and the subsequent plague started in 2039 in Buenos Aires. They are diametrically opposed to the SotD, but do not have the resources to fight them directly. The Bringers de La Entropía are responsible for a portion of the events that UP-KO is rumored to have committed. The Bringers de La Entropía still hold a large supply of chemical and biological weapons, and have been experimenting in genetic engineering and mental enhancement, some of the experiments have supposedly been successful.
About
Desert Crisis (DC) is a fun oriented Half-Life mod set in the close future. Realism definitely is not its main goal. Thus it's no surprise it features today's weapons (H&K, Sig, etc.) as well as e.g. laser pistols and plasma cannons. The same applies to the perks. These are abilities the players can choose from as part of a character selection menu that is very similar to the system heroes are generated by at some pen and paper RPG's.

This way it's possible to apply gaming styles different as day and night. While one player might choose no armor, enhanced melee powers, and a sledge hammer as main weapon the next one possibly goes for a very heavy suit, shield system, minigun combination. The "ninja" would then try to use his swiftness to attack the "tank" before the shields were up and the gun was firing massive amounts of slugs. It's one of DC's most remarkable accomplishments that both players have the same chances to be victories over each other. Nevertheless not all possibilities are equally easy to master especially since different players bring different skills and experiences with them.

To become good or even an expert in DC one first has to realize certain basics (of which there are quite a lot) and then needs to employ them through training. By the time one feels he got everything he can be pretty sure though that he forgot or lacks the practice concerning something he once used to know and be able to do. As a result the learning process has no end to it and often players specialize themselves over time. Working together these specialists can form crews that have what it takes to be successful in DC: calm cappers, tech gurus to activate short cuts, warriors to cover and defend them, as well as some lone mad men quick and skillful enough to be behind the enemies - always. Fortunately the common two or three gamers per server that join to just frag as much as possible are only seldomly needed to decide which team wins.

While there also are pure death match maps with limited respawns per player, usually DC maps either have a system of capture points to battle over or have multiple mission goals. A nice example is a map with a research facility which one team defends while the attacking team needs to destroy a satellite dish, to then capture a security computer, and steal a secret weapon. The design of the maps paces up the whole game even more as there hardly are any camping sites at all but a lot of possible hot spots. Basically all positions can be reached from at least two different directions. Wall jumps, dashing, and flip jumps guarantee three dimensional movement of the finest kind. On the other hand DC requires amazingly few keys compared to the possibilities offered to the users .

In a nut shell DC simply shows how much thought and work has been put into it. After all, how many mods are out there that actually took care of details like customizable heads, at least two ways most weapons can be used, detachable limbs, or small porn pictures on the capture points' displays?
