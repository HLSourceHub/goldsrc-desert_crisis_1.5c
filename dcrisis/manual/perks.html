Perks
Bio-Regeneration
Bonuses:
-Regenerating Health

Restrictions:
-None

With the recent advances in nanotechnology, people have begun injecting themselves with armies of tiny robots to help the body repair itself in the event of trauma.
Enhanced Speed
Bonuses:
-Extra Adrenaline
-Better special moves (walljumps, flips)

Restrictions:
-No Heavy Armor
-No Chaingun, EM Sniper, Plasma, or Flame

Player move very fast. ZOOM ZOOM!
Kung Fu Mastery
Bonuses:
-Increased Adrenaline
-Attack while dashing
-Increased Melee Damage
-Free throwing knives

Restrictions:
-None

When ammo runs out, hand-to-hand fighting becomes the name of the game. Those with  Kung-fu Master get a bonus to their melee attack damage, as well as the ability to attack with a melee weapon while dashing. Dashing attacks are something everyone else won't be able to do, giving Kung-fu Masters a great advantage.
Sharpshooter
Bonuses:
-Aiming
-Free IR Goggles
-Passive accuracy increase

Restrictions:
-No special moves (dashing, walljumps, flips)

With increased training comes increased accuracy. Sharpshooters spend hours per day honing their aiming skills in hopes of someday getting to use them in battle. Unfortunately, all of this aiming practice has left their bodies weak, and unable to the do athletic moves of other soldiers.

Aiming is activated by pressing your item key. You will gain perfect accuracy for a short period, but you will not be able to maintain that level of concentration long, so aim quickly.
Stealth
Bonuses:
-No footstep sounds
-Invisible to normal IR

Restrictions:
-No heavy armor

For the soldier who wants to enter the battle unannounced by his footsteps, Stealth is the premium tool for flanking, sneaking up on snipers, and many other silent activities.
Tech
Bonuses:
-Faster Cap-times
-Free Enhanced IR Goggles

Restrictions
-Must be wearing armor

Some soldiers undergo special training for the more complicated activities that happen during a war. Things like hacking a computer network, working with high-tech gadgets, and other geeky things are the realm of the Tech Guru.

Some paths to objectives can only be opened by a tech guru. Use your objective info button to find them.
Wootastic
Bonuses:
-Increased Adrenaline
-Unlimited pistol ammo

Restrictions:
-Only pistols
-No medium or heavy armor

You get no primary weapon, but you get akimbopistols which have unlimited ammo but slightly reduced damage. You also have extra adrenaline points and special moves (except for dashing) are enhanced. Longer and higher flips and better wall jumps are the result. Wootastic doesn't use an equipment choice to get akimbo pistols. One must wear light armor, or no armor to use this perk.
Team specific
Shield system (usa)
Bonuses:
-Increased melee damage with shield on
-Protection from headshots with shield

Restrictions:
-None

A shield to provide extra protection for themodern soldiers in the US Army. It is great for staying alive without having to be slowed down by big bulky armor, and it slowly regenerates when the shield is turned off.

Active the shield with your item key. It passively drains energy while on, so don't turn it on unless you need it. While the shield is up, damage will be delt to your perk energy instead of your armor and life.


 Antigrav System (upko)

This enables the user to jump over buildings in a single bound, among other things. When activated with one's item key, it enhances the jumping ability of the user and eliminates damage incurred when impacting the ground at high speeds.

All of this takes energy though, so the meter must be monitored closely. The meter takes time to recharge once depleted.
