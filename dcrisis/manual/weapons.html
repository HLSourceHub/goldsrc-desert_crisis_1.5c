Weapons
Primary Weapons
Mp5
Your average machine gun, has single, three round and full auto. Quite accurate for a SMG. Has single shot, three round burst and fully automatic modes. There is no secondary fire.
Mp5K Akimbo
The ultimate SMG. Fast, and deadly. Great for spraying and praying. Changing modes switches between which mp5k(s) you have out.
HK 33KA2 Sniper Rifle
The assault rifle. This gun has single, three round and full auto as well as a scope. This gun is very flexible and when wielded by an experienced user can be useful in any situation, be it sniping or close quarters battles. There is no secondary fire.
Franchi Spas 12
The good ol' shotgun.
Remington 12 Gauge + Akimbo Shotgun
This is a sawed off auto-loading 12 gauge shotgun. This is great for close quarters battles and when wielded correctly can fight off entire squads at a choke point. NOT for long or even medium ranges. This is the "stick it up their ass and pull the trigger" gun. Watch 'em fly. Secondary fire will fire BOTH barrels but it is very inaccurate and takes awhile to fire your next shot.
M61A1 Vulcan Chaingun
The minigun, 20mm shells of death being spewed at an insane rate. Don't plan on sniping anything with this, as it is dreadfully inaccurate. Great for suppressive fire though. There is no secondary fire.
M79 Grenade Launcher
The M79 40mm grenade launcher. This is great for suppressive fire or clearing out those enemy bunkers. Don't count on having the grenades sail forever though, they have a limited range. Also, be careful where you fire, as they can kill you to. Primary fire explodes on contact, secondary is a five second timed grenade.
Sig P220 akimbo
Rather fast RoF but not as deadly as the Desert Eagle.
Desert Eagle .357 akimbo
Slower, but significantly more powerful than the Sig.
Pulse Rifle
Another great multi-purpose gun. This is a machine gun and a grenade launcher in one. The pulse rifle can only fire in full-auto mode, there is no single shot or three round burst mode, yet the plus to it is it's built in grenade launcher. Press secondary fire and watch your enemies explode. Though once again, be careful. This gun can be worthless in the hands of a newbie.
Plasma Cannon
The plasma rifle fires super heated gas in either a concentrated blast, or a stream. When a concentrated blast is fired, the plasma streaks towards it's point of impact, and explodes. You have to be VERY careful as your plasma rifle can easily overheat and explode, killing you. Secondary fire fires a stream of plasma which is great for close ranges but it is VERY easy to overheat this.
Laser Rifle
Shoots with two modes automatic laser fire and pulse fire (shotgun type laser shots).
Raging Bull Akimbo
Powerfull gun, most damage least bullets.
HK USP Match Akimbo
FUTAR silenced handgun, least damage most bullets.
Laser Pistol Akimbo
Shoots laser blasts, can be charged by holding the fire button for shots that do more damage.  It also doesn't have ammo but a charge bar in place of clips.  This gun is reasonably accurate.
EM Sniper Rifle
The ultimate sniping weapon. This weapon has a rather low RoF compared to many of the other guns, but where it lacks in speed, it more than makes up for in power. When this weapon fires, it instantly reaches it's target and not only burns the flesh where it hits, but the laser is so powerful and concentrated that the air near the impact point becomes super heated and temporarily reaches a plasma state. This will severely damage the person and it is almost a guaranteed kill. This can take out anyone with a well placed shot.  Not for people who can't aim though, as your aim counts. Secondary fire zooms in completely instantly (Q3-style).
Flamethrower
The flame thrower is great for pushing the enemy back or clearing out tight areas. This weapon will light those it comes into contact with long enough on fire, they either have to get to water, or wait for it to go out on its own, the choice is theirs. In tight quarters, the effects of the flame thrower will increase since it will be "contained" by the walls and be more concentrated and the intensity of the heat will be significantly higher.

Melee Weapons
Akimbo Machetes
Hack Away
Big Hit Knife
A knife which can be slashed with and thrown as well.
Katana
Fast and deadly. Wild swinging with this is not effective though. Choose your strikes wisely.

Throwing Knives

Items
Grenade
Frag grenades are nothing more than your standard grenade. They explode and kill things, be careful with them.  The secondary fire sets the grenades to a five second timer as soon as you press fire, so don't hold onto it too long.
SMAW
One shot rocket launcher.
C4
These can be placed on any surface and triggered to explode. Great for breaching doors or protecting an entrance ("hello there enemy squad." *BOOM*).  Secondary fire will put out an addition pack of c4.
Alarm
Alarms are placed upon any wall or surface (besides water + such obviously). When the alarm's invisible beam is broken the alarm will sound alerting whoever placed it that their alarm has been tripped (a message is sent with the location). Alarms can be destroyed if you spot them.
FlareGun
Great for lighting up areas, toss one, and it'll be lit up. Nice for marking entrances to your base so people can't sneak in through the darkness. They are also great for diversionary tactics.  This is also great for diversions.
