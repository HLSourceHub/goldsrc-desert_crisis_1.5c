Coastline is a map set on the United States coastline where UP-KO is launching an
invasion. The USA is trying to prevent the UP-KO from doing so.

USA: Capture the mansion, the USA beach, barn, the house and the UP-KO beach 
in that order and stop the UP-KO team from capturing those points in the 
opposite order.

UP-KO: Capture the UP-KO beach, the house, the barn, the USA beach 
and the mansion in that order, and stop the USA team from capturing those
points in the opposite order.
