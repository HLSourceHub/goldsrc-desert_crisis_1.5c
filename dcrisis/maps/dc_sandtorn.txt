A remote desert village is under heavy UPKO assault.  
Their goal is to destroy a factory which has been 
creating a new form of Shield system for USA, amongst 
other prototypes.

UPKO: There is a bomb hidden in a crate defended by 
the new shield system.  You must disable the shield 
on the crate from the two computers located within 
the market.  Afterwards, destroy the crate, take 
the bomb inside, and plant it in front of the factory.

USA: You must stop UPKO at all costs, this city must 
not fall under UPKO rule.
