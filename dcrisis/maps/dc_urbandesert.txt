Urban Desert is a map set in a city in the middle east, the USA and UP-KO
are fighting for control of the city.

USA: Capture the tunnels, the embassy, the market, the church
and the compound, in that order and stop the UP-KO team from capturing
those points in the opposite order.

UP-KO: Capture the compound, the church, the market, the embassy and
the tunnels in that order and stop the USA team from capturing those points
in the opposite order.
