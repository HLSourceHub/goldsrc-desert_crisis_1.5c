This is a small team deathmatch map. It is best
with 4 or so players. It is a map set in an
Italian village that has been partially damaged.

Objectives: Kill the people that don't look like
you. :D

This map is played in rounds (the players become spectators
when they die 3 times, they are spectators until the round 
restarts).
